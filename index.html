Our Love Story

In 2021, Aurora came to Dublin for her Erasmus stay, Luiz was already there, already calling the city home. They met, they got along, and then they got along very well. By the time the stay was up, leaving didn’t make much sense anymore. Four years later, they’re both still in Dublin, still together and getting married, proof that some detours turn out to be exactly where you were supposed to end up.
